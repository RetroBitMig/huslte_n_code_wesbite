Why we serve young men of color

We inspire young at risk Black and Brown boys to become technology entrepreneurs. These boys are at the highest risk of dropping out of HS and being incarcerated.

Participate in the Summer Coding Leadership Academy (SCLA) & ongoing programs throughout the school year.

Become a SEAL, a coach in training for SCLA.

Attend college & potentially major in STEM.

Return to TXT during college to intern as a paid coach for SCLA.

Graduate college with degrees in STEM.

Successfully compete in the STEM job market & secure a career.

Give back to TXT as a volunteer or donor to invest in the next generation of leaders.

the cycle of reciprocity
