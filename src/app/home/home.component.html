rise up for upward mobility

Do you have a great idea for a website or app? Have you ever wondered how your favorite websites and apps work? Come learn and let your ideas run wild at our 5th annual South LA Hustle N’ Code Hackathon! Win a laptop, Beats headphones and more for your team’s brilliant idea. With great prizes, cool giveaways and great music, you don’t want to miss this exciting event! Innovate with us to solve community problems using code and hack away to build web and mobile apps. Your app could help improve your church, school, block, park, or any part of your community. All you have to bring is a hungry mind that is willing to learn and work with others. No coding experience is required.
